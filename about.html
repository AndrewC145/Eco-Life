About Us

How we started

Created in Markham, Ontario, we started as a small group of individuals with a simple goal in mind, to encourage others to develop environmentally sustainable habits.

From the beginning, it was tough branching out of our comfort zone to create Eco-life, but we took every opportunity that was given, whether it be at local events and communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for individuals to learn about the importance of environmental sustainability and to encourage them to take action in their daily lives.

We believe that by educating individuals about the importance of environmental sustainability, we can create a greener and healthier planet for future generations.
