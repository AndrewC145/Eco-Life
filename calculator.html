Your Carbon Footprint

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases, such as carbon dioxide and methane, generated by your actions, and thus, contribute to global warming and climate change.

In other words, it's a way to describe the environmental impact of man-made actions, hoping to support individuals and organizations to become more sustainable in the future.

There are several actions that contribute to your carbon footprint such as:

Transportation: Driving, Flying
Heating: Oil and gas
Food: Animal based products such as red meat and dairy
Waste: Throwing trash away
